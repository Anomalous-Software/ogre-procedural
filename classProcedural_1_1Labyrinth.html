Detailed Description

Creates a labyrinth structured image.

Creates a labyrinth structure from a specified perlin noise on a coloured background.

Example:

Procedural::TextureBuffer bufferLabyrinth(256);
Procedural::Labyrinth(&bufferLabyrinth).process();
